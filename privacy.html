Our Privacy Policy

What data do you store on me?

NAIL stores data on users in order to punish people violating our Terms of Service
The following is every piece of data stored on you:

  • Prompts, Time, Results, and the Message ID of every generation you attempt to make.
  • The amount of generations you have made.
  • Your Discord ID.

How are you protecting this data?

Every piece of data is stored in a single database that only I have access to.
Data relating to your Discord ID may be shared to Discord if you attempt to generate illegal content.
This data is not kept permanently and will be deleted after a days.
